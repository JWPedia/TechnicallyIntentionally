About

Hello, World! I am Jeewoong "Skaliq" Chang, a computer science student in the University of Rochester.

This website is built on the principle that no code trumps no code. It is written in plain html and hosted on Github Pages. No fancy styling nor animations. Fast, minimal, responsive.

I write about a wide range of interests spanning computer science and technology, biological sciences, media criticism, competitive Hearthstone, and deliberate lifestyle design.

You can also find me on other platforms, such as:

  • Instagram: Seldomly used for artworks and shoddy wildlife iPhoneography.
  • Anilist: Anime and manga I watched.
  • Naver: This Korean blog was mainly about entomology and hobbyist exotic pet breeding. It is no longer updated, as I fell out of the hobby. All posts are still available.
  • IRL: I know, it's hard to believe. To much surprise to us basement-dwelling hermits, the "real life" expansion is not a hoax. While not my native habitat, I do venture out into it every once in a while.

While I find it an extraneously difficult to imagine someone stealing content, just in case if you want to: do whatever you want with whatever on this website, so as long as you site your source.
