Ongoing: On Ultralearning Computer Science (2019)

One of my favorite bloggers Scott H Young recently condensed years of his research and writing down to a book, Ultralearning. It came out at an opportune moment, as I switched over to a CS major just two months ago, and the book features lengthy case studies of computer science ultralearing projects. As a late-comer to the field, it is imperative that I employ effective learning strategies to rapidly advance my skill level in order to catch up.

The goal of this tentative project is to accelerate me past the drudgery of mandatory first-year CS courses alongside regular university curriculum. It is currently at a research and design phase.
